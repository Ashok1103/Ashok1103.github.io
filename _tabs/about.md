I am a third-year Physics and Astronomy student at the University of 
Waterloo, completing a Computing minor alongside my degree. My physics 
background gives me a foundation in linear algebra, calculus, numerical 
methods, and statistical inference that I apply directly to machine 
learning and data science problems.

I build end-to-end ML projects: a galaxy morphology classifier using 
ResNet-18 and Grad-CAM that reached 90% accuracy on 17,736 astronomical 
images, and an enrollment prediction pipeline on real business data that 
taught me more about data ceilings than model tuning ever could. Both 
projects are documented on my 
[GitHub](https://github.com/Ashok1103).
